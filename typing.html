💡 Tips for Better Typing

🏠
Home Row
Place fingers on ASDF and JKL; — this is the home position.
👀
Look at the Screen
Try not to look at your hands. Look at the text to copy.
🎯
Accuracy First
Focus on typing correctly. Speed will improve naturally.
📅
Practice Daily
Even 5–10 minutes every day makes a big difference.
✋
Take Breaks
Rest your hands every 20–30 minutes if they get tired.
😌
Relax
Keep your shoulders and hands relaxed — no need to tense up!
