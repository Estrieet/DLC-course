Step-by-Step Lessons

12 clear and simple lessons. Learn at your own pace. Take as long as you need. No rush!

ⓘ 12 lessons covering everything from turning on a computer to sending emails. Each lesson takes about 15–20 minutes. Click to browse all lessons.

Typing Practice

Improve your typing speed and accuracy with interactive exercises. Start slowly and build up!

ⓘ Practice typing with real text passages. See your words-per-minute (WPM) speed and accuracy improve over time. Start slow — accuracy matters more than speed!

Quiz & Test

Test what you have learned after each lesson. Get instant feedback to help you remember.

Track Progress

See how far you have come! Track your lessons, quiz scores, and earn achievement badges.

ⓘ All your progress is saved automatically on your device. See your lesson completions, quiz scores, typing improvements, and achievements — all in one place.
