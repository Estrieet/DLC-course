Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions and learn how to use the course

❓ How do I start learning?

Click on "Lessons" in the navigation menu to see all available lessons. Start with Lesson 1 and complete each lesson at your own pace. You can take breaks anytime and come back later.

💾 How is my progress saved?

Your progress is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. This means your data stays on your device and no information is sent to any server.

🌙 How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Click the moon/sun icon (🌙/☀️) in the top navigation bar. Your preference will be saved automatically.

⌨️ What is the typing practice for?

The typing practice helps you improve your keyboard skills. It measures your typing speed (WPM - Words Per Minute) and accuracy. Regular practice will help you become faster and more accurate.

📊 Where do I track my progress?

Click on "Progress" in the navigation menu to see your overall course progress, completed lessons, quiz scores, and achievements.

🏆 What are achievements?

Achievements are badges you earn by completing milestones in the course. Complete lessons, pass quizzes, and improve your typing skills to unlock all achievements!

❌ I need more help

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please contact your teacher or instructor. They can provide personalized support.
