Help & FAQ

How do I unlock lessons?

Complete the quiz at the end of each lesson with a score of 70% or higher. This unlocks the next lesson automatically.

Is my progress saved?

Yes — your progress is saved automatically in your browser using LocalStorage and IndexedDB. No account or internet required.

How does the typing game work?

Click Start, then type the displayed text as accurately as possible. Your Words Per Minute (WPM) and accuracy are calculated using a WebAssembly module for speed.

Can I change the theme?

Yes! Click the sun/moon icon in the top bar to switch between dark and light mode. Your preference is remembered.

What if I need to start over?

Go to the Teacher page and click "Reset Student Progress", or ask your teacher to do it for you.

What technologies does this use?

HTML5, CSS3 (glassmorphism, animations), Vanilla JavaScript, WebAssembly (WASM, WAT source included), IndexedDB, LocalStorage, and SessionStorage — all without any external libraries or npm.
